Biography

Ferhat Ozgur Catak is a Chief Researcher of Cyber Security Institute at the TUBITAK-BILGEM. His research interests include distributed machine learning, cyber security and deep learning.

Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine/Deep Learning Methods for Cyber-Security
  • Distributed machine learning
  • Homomorphic encryption based Privacy-preserving machine learning

Education
